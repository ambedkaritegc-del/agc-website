About This Event

This in-person meetup is designed for professionals at all stages of their career. Whether you are looking for your first job, trying to move to a better company, or want to give back by mentoring others — this is the space for you. The evening includes structured speed networking, career talks from 3 community members who have navigated tough career transitions, and open 1:1 time.
