About AGC

A Community Built on
Mutual Support
and Shared Progress

The Ambedkarite Global Council is a non-political community inspired by the vision of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar — connecting students, professionals, and entrepreneurs worldwide.

Join the Community → Meet the Members
Our Mission

Building Pathways for Global Success

AGC connects Ambedkarites worldwide to help each other access education, grow careers, and build businesses. We believe that collective action is the most powerful force for social progress.

Founded on the philosophy of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, Jyotirao Phule, Savitribai Phule, and Shahu Maharaj — we turn inspiration into action through community.

Core Philosophy

Everyone Gives & Everyone Receives

AGC is not a charity and not a job board. It is a mutual support network. Every person who joins brings something — and gets something in return.

🎁

What Members Give

Job referrals and professional introductions
Study abroad guidance and scholarship info
Mentoring in their field or profession
Business advice and startup support
Legal, financial, and technical knowledge
Community events and workshops
and
🤲

What Members Receive

Access to a trusted global network
Career guidance and job opportunities
Study and scholarship support
Business mentors and co-founders
Connections to diaspora communities abroad
Community events and learning sessions

Our Values

What We Stand For

⚖️

Equality

Every member is equal regardless of profession, income, or location. We do not discriminate.

🤝

Mutual Support

We believe in reciprocal help. No one is just a giver or just a receiver — everyone is both.

📚

Education First

We see education as the most important tool for lasting social change and individual progress.

🌍

Global Thinking

Our community is borderless. We connect people across India, Europe, America, and beyond.

🚫

Non-Political

AGC does not align with any political party. We are a community platform, not a political movement.

🔓

Open Community

Anyone who shares our values of education, mutual respect, and uplift is welcome to join.
